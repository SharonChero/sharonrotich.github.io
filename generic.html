About Me

A results driven learner working towards getting well-developed knowledge on building and maintaining data in today’s business applications.

Hello, my name is Sharon and I currently work remotely as a freelance financial data analyst. I hold a bachelor’s degree Business, with a specialization in Finance from the Cooperative University of Kenya. With a business and finance background, I have grown a great deal of interest in information technology, precisely the concept of big data and it role harnessing valuable insights for better business performance. I am fascinated by the concept of collecting, cleaning and using data to create valuable machine learning models and financial models that can be used in generating beneficial insights for business and financial institutions. I am well versed with Python, Java, R-programming, Stata SQL, Excel, Tableau and Power BI.
